“And what will you leave to your bairns and wife? 
Edward, Edward, and what will you leave to your bairns and wife 
When you're gone out to sea, oh?”

“The world is round, let them beg through life.
Mother, mother, the world is round, let them beg to life.”

“And what about your mother dear? 
Edward, Edward, and what about your mother dear?
My son, my son, that tell to me, oh.”

“The curse of hell upon thee, 
Mother mother, the curse of hell upon thee. 
But you have counseled me!”
