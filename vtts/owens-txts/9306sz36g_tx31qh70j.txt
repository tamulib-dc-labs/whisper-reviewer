“On this place I own a lease, sir, I am justice of the peace, sir.
The law west of the Pecos all is here.
And you've acted very badly.” So the dude, he went off sadly.
While down his lily cheeks there rolled a tear.

Now you boys have heard the story of Roy Bean in all his glory.
He was the man who was the justice of the law.
He was handy with his hook, sir. He was ornery in his look, sir.
And just now I ain't a-tellin' anymore.
